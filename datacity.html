DataCity models a typical job posting platform with the added benefit of candidates leaving precise reviews to add objectivity and transparency to the interview process for software and data engineering roles.

This product displays how this can be accomplished with certain parties, such as Human Resources and Hiring Managers, having access to particular features of the product while at the same time providing job seekers an advantage by having the ability to view other candidatesâ€™ experiences.
