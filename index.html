Intro

Hello, I’m a student at UHD studying computer science (CS). I became interested in CS after wanting to understand the software and code behind Bitcoin in 2015. Initially I tried learning on my own by taking a Bitcoin course through Coursera. The concepts were foreign and difficult to grasp. One day after watching a financial news show, I had what could be called an aha moment. I knew that I needed to understand Bitcoin and enrolled in an introductory CS course. It has been an interesting journey since then. While working at a call center in 2015 I would talk to my neighbors about Bitcoin. Most of them had not heard about Bitcoin and were eager to listen. Now, Bitcoin has become a household name. I’ve worked with a group on a cryptocurrency project and have seen the trends, rises, and falls of cryptocurrencies. Bitcoin to me and to a lot of people appears to be the strongest and most promising cryptocurrency. Studying CS has exposed me to many different avenues that one can pursue such as AI/Machine Learning, Web Development, and Software Engineering. Though Bitcoin was the spark that attracted me into this field, I’m still trying to discover what I’ll be good at and where my position will be in the industry once I graduate and become employable.
